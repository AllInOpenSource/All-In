Why are we developing this resource?

One of the most common challenges we heard during the Maintainers Listening Tour was not knowing where to start when considering ways to advance diversity and inclusion within open source communities.

The number of DEI resources we have is overwhelming. We wish there was a website or repo where we could go to get all of the resources in one place.

The All In for Maintainers DEI Resource Hub is a digital archive dedicated to sharing resources addressing DEI in open source research and trends. Our goal is to help share information that will support maintainers and community leaders in their journey of learning how to create more diverse and inclusive open source communities.

Whether you are taking the first steps in exploring how to make open source projects more accessible and inclusive or far into your DEI journey and seeking “what’s next,” we are here to help every step of the way.
